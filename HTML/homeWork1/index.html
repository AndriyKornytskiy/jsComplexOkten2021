Arnold Schwarzenegger

Arnold Alois Schwarzenegger was born in Thal on July 30, 1947, the second son of Aurelia (née Jadrny) and Gustav Schwarzenegger. His mother was of Czech descent, while his paternal great-grandfather, Wenzel Mach, was also Czech and came from the village of Chocov near Mladá Vožice. Wenzel had a child out of wedlock with Kunigunde Schwarzenegger, and the child (Schwarzenegger's paternal grandfather) was originally named Carl Mach but later adopted his mother's surname Schwarzenegger.
